By some miracle, we made it through Milton with little more than a couple power flickers and a broken fence.[^1]

We have a bit of a habit of faring well through hurricanes -- when Ian ravaged our area 2 years ago, our house somehow managed to stay completely dry even though the road was (allegedly[^2]) 6 feet underwater. Milton, thankfully, continued this trend.

[^1]: The fence in question wasn't serving a useful purpose anyway, so that's probably fine -- our main fence got through unscathed.
[^2]: We decided to evacuate, so I didn't see any of this myself.
